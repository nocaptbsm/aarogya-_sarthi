How Aarogya Sarthi Helps

Designed to address the most critical public health communication needs with simple, conversational solutions.

Symptom Guidance

Users can describe their symptoms in their own language. The chatbot provides verified, preliminary information from government health sources and advises on the appropriate level of care (e.g., "This sounds like something you should discuss at your local PHC."), without ever providing a diagnosis.

Vaccination Reminders

Parents can register their child's birth date to receive automated reminders for the entire vaccination schedule as per the National Immunization Programme. The chatbot can also provide directions to the nearest vaccination centers.

Outbreak Alerts

Integrated with state health databases, Aarogya Sarthi can send out geo-targeted alerts in the event of a disease outbreak (like Dengue or Cholera), providing actionable preventive measures and official advisories in the local language.

Technical Architecture

A modern, scalable, and secure stack built for reliability and nationwide deployment.

Core Technology Stack

  • AI/NLP: Python, TensorFlow, fine-tuned LLMs (e.g., Llama 3, Gemma) for high-quality conversational AI.
  • Backend: Node.js/Python (FastAPI) for robust, scalable REST APIs.
  • Messaging: WhatsApp Cloud API & Twilio for SMS fallback.
  • Database: PostgreSQL for structured data and user logs.

Integration & Infrastructure

  • Translation: Real-time multilingual support via dedicated translation APIs.
  • Hosting: Cloud-native deployment on AWS/Azure with auto-scaling.
  • Security: End-to-end encryption, data minimization, and compliance with data protection laws.
  • Monitoring: Real-time analytics dashboards for performance and usage insights.
